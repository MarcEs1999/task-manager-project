Task Manager Personas

Persona 1: Sofia Torres

Persona Type: Full-time Professional

Age: 30

Location: Miami, FL

Technical Comfort: Proficient with technology

Profession: Marketing Manager

Backstory

Sofia works as a marketing manager for a mid-sized company. She manages her team’s campaigns, content calendars, and reports, and uses a task manager tool to stay organized.

She often shifts priorities based on client needs, requiring a flexible and easy-to-update tool.

Frustrations

  • Difficulty integrating tools with other systems like email and calendars.
  • Lack of real-time collaboration features.
  • Overly complicated interfaces that reduce productivity.

Motivations

  • Track multiple projects and see the status at a glance.
  • Assign tasks to team members and monitor progress.
  • Integration with email and calendars for efficiency.

Ideal Experience

A seamless task manager that allows quick updates, easy collaboration, and smooth integration with other tools. Sofia needs a dashboard that shows all projects and their status without switching screens.

Persona 2: Michael Green

Persona Type: Graduate Student

Age: 22

Location: New York City, NY

Technical Comfort: Tech-savvy

Profession: Full-time student, part-time intern

Backstory

Michael is a computer science graduate student interning at a tech company. He manages his academic and work tasks using a task manager tool.

He prefers tools that sync across devices, as he uses both a laptop and phone throughout the day.

Frustrations

  • Lack of mobile-friendly interfaces or syncing across devices.
  • Rigid task structures that don’t allow re-prioritization based on changing deadlines.

Motivations

  • Track school assignments and internship tasks with reminders and deadlines.
  • Tag tasks with priority levels and easily switch between academic and work lists.

Ideal Experience

A flexible task manager that allows him to switch between school and work responsibilities. He values tools with task-sharing features, especially for group projects at school.
